# AWS KMS
> Source: https://docs.aws.amazon.com/whitepapers/latest/aws-overview/security-services.html
> Collected: 2026-04-09
> Published: Unknown

AWS Key Management Service (AWS KMS) makes it easy for you to create and
   manage cryptographic keys and control their use across a wide range of AWS services and in your
   applications. AWS KMS uses hardware security modules (HSM) to protect and validate your AWS KMS keys
   under the FIPS 140-2 Cryptographic Module Validation Program.  AWS KMS is integrated with AWS CloudTrail
   to provide you with logs of all key usage to help meet your regulatory and compliance
   needs.
