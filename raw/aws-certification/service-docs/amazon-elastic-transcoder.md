# Amazon Elastic Transcoder
> Source: https://docs.aws.amazon.com/whitepapers/latest/aws-overview/media-services.html
> Collected: 2026-04-09
> Published: Unknown

Amazon Elastic Transcoder is media transcoding in the
   cloud. It is designed to be a highly scalable, easy-to-use, and cost-effective way for developers
   and businesses to convert (or transcode) media files from their source format into versions that
   will play back on devices such as smartphones, tablets, and PCs.
